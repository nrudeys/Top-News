ABOUT


The New York Times, based in New York City, is a media organization that is well known for its daily newspaper. This website uses the New York Times (NYT) Top Stories API to get the current top World, US, Health, and techonology news articles. These News articles can further filtered by keyword, subsections, or time range. The purpose of this is to allow users to quickly find news articles that are of interest to them.
To keep the displayed results relevant, data is fetched from the NYT API every hour according to a session.
